Welcome to The Cuppa Corner

Welcome to The Cuppa Corner, where each sip tells a story and each scent generates feelings of love and camaraderie. Our cosy café, located in the dynamic city of Cluj-Napoca, Romania, is more than simply a location to get your favourite brew; it's a paradise for coffee connoisseurs and those looking for a nice retreat.
